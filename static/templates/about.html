How CityFix Works

Our streamlined process ensures that infrastructure issues are reported, tracked, and resolved efficiently with full transparency.

1

Report Issue

Citizens report infrastructure problems using our intuitive interface. Photos, location data, and detailed descriptions help create comprehensive reports.

2

Review & Assign

Municipal officials review reports, verify details, prioritize based on urgency, and assign them to appropriate maintenance teams.

3

Execute Work

Maintenance teams receive detailed work orders, complete the repairs, and document their work with before-and-after photos.

4

Verify & Close

Work is reviewed for quality, citizens are notified of completion, and the report is closed with full documentation of the resolution.
